RS Metropolitan Medical Center

MMC Hospital is the only private hospital in Indonesia which is supported by more than 30 sub-specialist doctors with the title of Professor and more than 200 specialists from various specialists with the development of medical science currently.

In order to provide customer satisfaction and comfort, MMC Hospital has a special floor for VIP/executive patient care (Sujudi Executive Ward) which is specially designed with three concepts, namely Patient Centered Care, Green Hospital and Modern Healing Center.

Since 2014, MMC Hospital has managed to get recognition from the Hospital Accreditation Commission (KARS) as a hospital that has met hospital accreditation standards and has passed the PARIPURNA LEVEL BINTANG LIMA
